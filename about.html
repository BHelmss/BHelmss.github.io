Background

I’m Brendan Helms, a cybersecurity enthusiast with a burning passion for decoding the complexities of digital security—and a drive to make my mark in the industry. My journey kicked off with a curiosity about how systems tick, which led me to earn my B.S. in Cybersecurity from the University of Central Missouri. Along the way, I’ve stacked up certifications and rolled up my sleeves on projects that fuel my excitement—like crafting SSH honeypots to trap intruders, building NIST-compliant password checkers to enforce ironclad standards, and digging into Splunk to analyze logs and sniff out threats.

I’m at home exploring network vulnerabilities with Kali Linux, scripting in Python to automate security tasks, and sharpening my skills in log ingestion and query-based analysis. My time at UCM honed my knack for secure coding and taught me to approach every challenge with a hacker’s mindset—always probing, always learning. Beyond the screen, I’m plugged into the latest cyber trends, eager to innovate and strengthen the digital world one solution at a time.
